Instagram

Social Media

Instagram is a photo and video sharing app where users post stories, reels, and feeds.

Pros
  • Can support art, photography, and visual storytelling in the curriculum.
  • Some educators and institutions use it for announcements and project showcases.
Cons
  • Infinite scroll and reels encourage passive consumption and time away from learning.
  • Ads and suggested content are not curated for educational use.
